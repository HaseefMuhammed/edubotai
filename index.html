About EDU BOT AI

EDU BOT AI - THE FEATURE OF LEANING.

EDU BOT AI is a smart virtual teacher designed to assist students in learning through AI-driven technology. Powered by a custom-built Edu Bot OS, it combines modern web technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, and Bootstrap. The system includes six apps, offering video calls, file sharing, live school updates, chat, and a voice assistant that answers student questions instantly. Teachers can control all features using the Edu Connect app, which also allows admin management and activity tracking. EDU BOT AI provides an interactive and modern approach to education, making learning more accessible and engaging.

Revolutionizing Learning with Smart Virtual Teaching

EB Stream

An interactive video call feature for real-time learning. Teachers can connect with students, share screens, and conduct live discussions.

  • Live video sessions with screen sharing
  • Instant doubt-clearing during video calls
  • "Inform Teacher" button for immediate alerts.

EB Files

A seamless platform for file sharing, enabling teachers to share educational resources instantly with students, enhancing classroom engagement.

  • Upload and display notes, videos, and PDFs
  • Real-time access to learning materials
  • Efficient file management for teachers and students

School Resources

This title highlights the platform's role in keeping students, teachers, and parents up-to-date with important news and school-related resources.

  • Real-Time Updates: Keep students, teachers, and parents informed with the latest school news and announcements.
  • Resource Access: Provide easy access to school resources such as timetables, event schedules, and important notices.
  • School Website Integration: Direct access to the school website for additional information and resources..

EB Models

This title highlights the platform's role in keeping students, teachers, and parents up-to-date with important news and school-related resources.

  • Exploring intricate systems like the respiratory or skeletal systems in a fully interactive environment.
  • Customizing views to focus on specific areas or components for deeper understanding.
  • Enhancing visualization of challenging topics, transforming abstract concepts into tangible, interactive experiences..

EB Doodle

Collaborative Drawing enables students and teachers to draw together in real time through the EDU Connect platform. This feature is perfect for:

  • Real-time interaction, allowing students and teachers to collaboratively draw and illustrate concepts during lessons.
  • Enhancing understanding by dynamically visualizing ideas, making abstract topics clearer and more engaging.
  • Saving and sharing drawings, so students can easily submit their work for feedback or review and access teacher illustrations at any time.

EB Lumin

EB Lumin is a creative platform where students explore crafts like origami, paper sculptures, and clay modeling through interactive tutorials, fostering skill development and self-expression.

  • Hands On Learning : Step-by-step guides make crafting accessible for solo or group activities.
  • Skill Enhancement : Activities boost fine motor skills, creativity, and problem-solving.
  • inspiring Community : Students can share their work, motivating peers and building confidence..

EB Attendance

EB ATTENDANCE is an RFID-based system for seamless attendance tracking, allowing students to tap RFID cards for instant logging and minimizing classroom interruptions.

  • Instant Attendance : Students tap RFID cards for quick, accurate attendance.
  • Easy Management : Teachers can access records and generate reports effortlessly.
  • Secure Data : Ensures accuracy and securely stores information, reducing paperwork.

EB Translate

EB Translate is a translation tool for multilingual classrooms, supporting over 15 languages to promote inclusivity and understanding.

  • Seamless Communication : Facilitates interaction between students and teachers of diverse language backgrounds.
  • Enhanced Learning : Enables full student participation by breaking down language barriers.
  • Clear Instructions : Allows teachers to provide instructions that all students can easily understand..

EB Chat

This title emphasizes how EB Chat supports real-time, interactive communication, fostering better learning through direct engagement between students and teachers.

  • Real-Time Messaging: Allows students to chat directly with teachers for instant communication and doubt clarification.
  • Group Discussions: Enables group conversations between students and teachers for collaborative learning.
  • History and Search: Easily access past chats and search for specific information or discussions.

EB Assistant

EDU AI is a voice assistant capable of answering any questions students may have. By simply asking, “Hey teacher, what is photosynthesis?”, students receive instant responses.

  • Voice recognition for student queries
  • Instant AI-powered answers for various topics
  • Accessible via all devices

Edu Connect

A companion app for teachers to manage EDU BOT AI. This app allows teachers to control the virtual teacher, engage with students, and monitor activities.

  • Move and control EDU BOT AI remotely
  • Join EB Stream and manage EB Files
  • Track activities and access passwords easily

Edu Mate

Edu Mate is a platform that keeps students connected to live classes and resources, enabling real-time interaction and continuous learning from anywhere.

  • Live Interaction : Attend classes and engage in real-time discussions from any location.
  • On-Demand Resources : Access lecture notes, study materials, and presentations anytime.
  • Seamless Communication : Chat with teachers for quick clarifications and academic support.
